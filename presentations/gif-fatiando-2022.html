About

In this talk I present some of the tools we have in Fatiando a Terra to the Geophysical Inversion Facility group from the Department of Earth, Ocean and Atmospheric Sciences, University of British Columbia.

This talk was based on the Fatiando a Terra: Open-source tools for geophysics talk.
