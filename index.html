Santiago Soler

About me

I'm a Lic. in Physics graduated from the Universidad Nacional de Rosario and now PhD Student in Geophysics at the Universidad Nacional de San Juan I research forward and inverse gravity models at the Instituto Geofísico Sismológico Volponi at the Computer-Oriented Geoscience Lab with the support of a CONICET scholarship. I'm also committed to contribute on open-source projects to improve science openness and reproducibility.
